Bin Jiang

Managing Partner

Bin Jiang is a seasoned deal-maker with over 18 years of experience in Investment Banking, Corporate M&A, Strategic Business Development and Technology Management. He has established a strong track record of multi-billion dollar deals in semiconductor investment and M&A activities. He was most recently Head of Corporate M&A and Investor Relations at Parade Technologies and earlier at Avago Technologies (now Broadcom Inc.). Prior to that he was an equity research analyst at Morgan Stanley and Citibank. In early day of his career, he was a hardware designer at Cypress Semiconductor.

He has an MBA degree from UC Berkeley, MS Engineering from University of Texas at Austin, and a BSEE from Tsinghua University.
