Hanxi Chen

Venture Partner

Hanxi Chen is a 20 year IT industry veteran. She has been leading various roles ranging from product marketing, business development to product management. She is high energy, strategic, passionate about products and customers and focused on big market trend and movements. She has also grown with the industry, from the chips/components in the 90s to system in 2000s and big data in the past few years.

In her earlier career, she has spent over 12 years at SUN Microsystems, the premier computer systems company during the 90s, moving around the company and growing to management roles. During this time, she had also made important connections with industry movers and shakers including many founders of thriving silicon Valley startups.

Hanxi has a Ph.D degree in Material Science from Penn State University.
