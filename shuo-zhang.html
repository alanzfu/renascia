Shuo Zhang

Managing Partner, CEO

Shuo Zhang is a high performance business leader with over 25 years of experience in general management, marketing, sales and strategic business development in the semiconductor industry.

She has extensive experience in driving leading edge IC technology adoption into mobile, computing, consumer and networking markets. She also established a strong track record of multi-billion dollar deals in semiconductor strategic M&A initiatives. She has held various management positions in LSI Logic, Altera, Agilent Technologies, Avago technologies, and Cypress Semiconductors Corporation.

She has an MS Engineering degree from Penn State University, BSEE from Zhejiang University, and an Executive Management degree from Stanford University
