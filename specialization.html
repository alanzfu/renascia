Tuck-in Acquisitions

Renascia partners with clients to find unique, "under-the-radar" acquisition targets. These niche tuck-ins, identified through our extensive network, bring crucial technologies or market access to complement existing portfolios.

Creative Carve Outs

Renascia works with clients to assess their non-core or troubled business units and find buyers that will most value those assets. These creative carve-outs leverage our domain experience to monetize rather than shutdown low performing businesses.

Bridging the Oceans

With vast working knowledge of the international markets, Renascia is uniquely positioned to surface investment and partnership opportunities overseas.

Many Asian and European firms have developed specialized semiconductor technologies or applications that are ripe for a strategic exit. China has an almost insatiable appetite for semiconductor investment. Renascia has the contacts, both within the industry and in many cases, within the state agencies, ministries and councils, to discover opportunities and get them closed.

Synergy Attainment

Renascia doesn't stop working for you when the LOI is signed or even when the deal is closed. Our deep experience with merger integration makes us specifically qualified to help our clients realize targeted synergies after the deal is done.

With extensive checklists and opportunity boards, we will help our partners find and execute on hidden value in strategic transactions. At Renascia, the deal is only done when we meet our client's objectives, and YOU say its done!
