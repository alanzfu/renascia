Dan McCranie

Senior Advisor

Dan McCranie is an advisor to Renascia, leveraging over 40 years of strategic semiconductor experience and a vast network to help guide the firm.

Dan serves as the Chairman of the Board of Directors of ON Semiconductor, and also serves on the Board of Directors of Mentor Graphics. He has served as Chairman of the Board of Freescale (recently sold to NXP). He previously was Executive Chairman of Virage Logic, from 2008 until Synopsys purchased it, in 2010.

He worked for Cypress Semiconductor from 1993 until 2001, and again in 2014 and 2015, where he was Executive Vice President of Sales and Applications. He also served on the board of directors of Cypress (which recently acquired Spansion).

He also served as Chairman of the Board of Actel, until it was sold to Microsemi, and of Xicor, until it was acquired by Intersil.
