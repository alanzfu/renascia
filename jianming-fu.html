Dr. Fu is an entrepreneur and an innovator. He has broad experience in photovoltaic and semiconductor device design, fabrication, and equipment, as well as business operation. He was successful in creating new technology, turning it into products and ramping mass manufacturing in both US and China. Dr. Fu is currently CTO and co-founder of Enova Technology, Inc, developing autonomous robotics for smart manufacturing. He is also working with VCâ€™s on investing and mentoring young entrepreneurs. Before this he was CTO and co-founder of Silevo, Inc. In Silevo, they developed and mass manufactured greatly innovative high efficiency solar cells and modules. The company was acquired in 2014 by SolarCity, a Tesla Motor company. Dr. Fu also especially has intensive experience in semiconductor/solar/thin film manufacturing equipment, made critical and highly commercially successful inventions in PVD technology while he held various key positions in Applied Materials including general manager of PVD Division.

Dr. Fu received Ph. D. in Physics from The Pennsylvania State University, MS in Semiconductor Physics in Institute of Semiconductors, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and B.S. in Physics from Wuhan University. He has been issued with over 80 US patents.
