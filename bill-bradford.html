Bill Bradford

Partner

  • 30+ years of semiconductor sales, marketing, GM and CEO Experience (Cypress, Entropic, Freescale, Minco ON Semi, TI)
  • Strategic Activity includes: Freescale Privatization, ON Semi Restructure, Entropic acquisition of Trident, Minco sale to Micross Components
  • BSEE Rose-Hulman, MS Mgmt University of Alabama, EDB GSU (in process)

Bill Bradford is a partner at Renascia, where he focuses on business development and strategy. He has spent nearly 30 years in the semiconductor industry, with extensive corporate sales and marketing executive leadership experience. He was most recently the president and CEO of Minco Technology Labs, which sold to Micross Components in early 2015. Prior to that he was senior vice president of sales for Entropic Communications, as it purchased Trident. He was the senior vice president of sales at Freescale during the leveraged buyout of that firm. He was the senior vice president of sales and marketing for ON Semi, where he was actively involved in the restructure of the company following its spinout from Motorola.

Bill has a BS in Electrical Engineering (& Economics Minor) from Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology, a Master of Science in Management from the University of Alabama), and is currently completing an Executive Doctorate in Business from Georgia State University.
